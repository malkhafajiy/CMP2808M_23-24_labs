Workshop 10 - Data Loss Prevention (hands-on task and discussion)

 

 

This week's focus in terms of a practical hands-on task for the topic of Cloud Computing Ethics is based on looking at data loss and ramifications of sensitive data being exposed.

In this workshop you will:

i) Learn about and apply the Data Loss Prevention API.

ii) Discuss with the workshop staff the possible issues with sensitive data being exposed.
